# ⚡ NBA Flow

A minimalist, high-performance productivity dashboard designed to help you organize your tasks, track your habits, and manage your time effectively. Find your focus, stay in your NBA Flow.

---

## ✨ Features

* **🎯 Task Management:** Track and organize your daily to-dos with a sleek, interactive UI.
* **⏱️ Focus Timer:** Built-in Pomodoro-style timer to maintain deep work and eliminate distractions.
* **📊 Habit Tracking:** Monitor your daily habits and consistency directly from the dashboard.
* **📈 Activity Analytics:** Beautifully integrated charts to visualize your productivity progress over time.
* **🎨 Minimalist Dark UI:** Easy on the eyes, featuring custom-designed premium dark palettes with Inter and DM Mono typography.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* **HTML5 & CSS3** - Custom semantic structure and premium dark mode styling.
* **JavaScript (ES6+)** - Core application logic, timer mechanics, and dynamic UI rendering.
* **Chart.js** - For sleek, responsive productivity graphs and data visualization.
* **Google Fonts** - Inter & DM Mono for modern, readable typography.
